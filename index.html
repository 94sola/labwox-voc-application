ANALYSIS OF VOLATILE ORGANIC COMPOUNDS IN DUMPSITE SOIL

Headspace GCFID


Introduction

VOCs are an important markers of soil contamination. They are widely used in many industries as solvents and chemical reagents. VOCs leach into soils at landfills and dumpsites leading to soil contamination.


Samples

A sample of dumpsite soil obtained from a landfill.


Sample Preparation

2g of soil samples were transferred into a 20mL headspace vial and 20mL of HPLC grade methanol was added to the vial. The vial was then crimped and placed in the Sampler for analysis


Calibration

VOCs standard, 2000ppm (Catalog Number: M-502A-R-10X) was purchased from AccuStandard (USA). Four (4) point serial dilution calibration standards (2.0, 0.5, 0.25, 0.12mg/L) was prepared from the stock and used to calibrate the GC


Instrument Conditions (Agilent 8860 GC-FID/7697 Headspace Sampler)



Inlet EPC Split/splitless
Mode Split

Column Agilent DB624(0.53mm * 30 m x 3 μm)

Carrier gas Helium

Oven Temperature (7697) 80°C
